Proprietary methodology

TPA — Think, Plan and Action

A proprietary methodology designed to turn communication into strategy.

THINK

Think before executing

Before any content is created, the agency enters a deep stage of analysis and study to understand the business, the audience and the desired positioning.

  • Persona research
  • Competitive analysis
  • Brand pillars
  • SWOT analysis
  • Current business moment
  • Content DNA
  • Editorial lines
  • Tone of voice
  • Brand personality
PLAN

Building the strategic path

After the analysis, the agency develops a clear execution plan designed to take the brand from where it is to where it wants to go.

  • Content strategies
  • Campaigns
  • Timelines
  • Creative directions
  • Communication formats
  • Growth strategies
  • Execution planning
ACTION

Execution with continuous monitoring

Once the strategic structure is defined, the plan comes to life through production, publishing, campaigns, management and performance analysis.

  • Content production
  • Publishing
  • Campaigns
  • Social media management
  • Results tracking
  • Monthly meetings
  • Strategic improvements
  • Seasonal actions
